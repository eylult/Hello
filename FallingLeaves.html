Falling Leaves

Yes, they do. The leaves fall, at a certain time, certain month of the year. A month which I wasn't born in, luckily.

There is a connection though. I feel it in my bones; coming, coming, coming... I have never waited for it with an excitement, I don't think I ever will. Then one asks; "Why are so pale close to the end, like the falling leaves?"


Why would I open the maze of thoughts in my mind to you? You would get lost, and I wouldn't bother to help.. If you are insisting though, ask away eylultasyurek@yahoo.com
